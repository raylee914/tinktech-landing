SC04 Permutations and Combinations, Progressions 练习题

SC04 Permutations and Combinations, Progressions Practice Question

9. (a) 下图有 $A$、$B$、$C$、$D$、$E$ 和 $F$ 六点。 (i) 若从中选出两点连成线段，一共有多少条线段？ (ii) 若从中选出三点连成三角形，共可绘画出多少个三角形？ (b) 已知 $\{a_n\}$ 为等差数列，若数列的第三项为 1，首四项之和为 0，求： (i) 第六项之值， (ii) 首 20 项之和。

📌 考点：Permutations and Combinations, Progressions · 难度：中等

📌 Topic: Permutations and Combinations, Progressions · Difficulty: 中等

✅ 答案

✅ Answer

(a)(i) 15; (a)(ii) 20; (b)(i) 7; (b)(ii) 320

✏️ 完整解题步骤

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

1
For part (a)(i): We need to choose 2 points from 6 to form a line segment. This is calculated using combinations: $N = {}^6C_2 = \frac{6 \times 5}{2 \times 1} = 15$.
2
For part (a)(ii): We need to choose 3 points from 6 to form a triangle. Assuming no three points are collinear, the number of triangles is $N = {}^6C_3 = \frac{6 \times 5 \times 4}{3 \times 2 \times 1} = 20$.
3
For part (b), let $a$ be the first term and $d$ be the common difference of the arithmetic progression $\{a_n\}$.
4
From the given information, $a_3 = a + 2d = 1$ (Equation 1).
5
From $S_4 = 0$, we have $\frac{4}{2}[2a + (4-1)d] = 0 \Rightarrow 2(2a + 3d) = 0 \Rightarrow 4a + 6d = 0$ (Equation 2).
6
Solving Equation 1 and Equation 2: From (1), $a = 1 - 2d$. Substitute into (2): $4(1 - 2d) + 6d = 0 \Rightarrow 4 - 8d + 6d = 0 \Rightarrow -2d = -4 \Rightarrow d = 2$.
7
Substitute $d = 2$ into (1): $a + 2(2) = 1 \Rightarrow a = -3$.
8
For (b)(i), find the sixth term: $a_6 = a + 5d = -3 + 5(2) = -3 + 10 = 7$.
9
For (b)(ii), find the sum of the first 20 terms: $S_{20} = \frac{20}{2}[2a + (20-1)d] = 10[2(-3) + 19(2)] = 10[-6 + 38] = 10(32) = 320$.
