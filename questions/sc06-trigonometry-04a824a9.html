SC06 Trigonometry Practice Question

12. 已知 $A, B$ 在同一象限, 且 $\sin A = -\frac{3}{5}, \cos B = \frac{12}{13}$。求 $\sin(A + B)$ 的值。 A $-\frac{56}{65}$ B $-\frac{16}{65}$ C $\frac{16}{65}$ D $\frac{56}{65}$

📌 考点：Trigonometry · 难度：中等

📌 Topic: Trigonometry · Difficulty: 中等

✅ 答案

✅ Answer

A

✏️ 完整解题步骤

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

1
$\sin A = -\frac{3}{5}$ implies $A$ is in Quadrant III or IV.
2
$\cos B = \frac{12}{13}$ implies $B$ is in Quadrant I or IV.
3
Since $A$ and $B$ are in the same quadrant, they must both be in Quadrant IV.
4
In Quadrant IV, $\cos A$ is positive and $\sin B$ is negative.
5
$\cos A = \sqrt{1 - \sin^2 A} = \sqrt{1 - (-\frac{3}{5})^2} = \sqrt{1 - \frac{9}{25}} = \frac{4}{5}$.
6
$\sin B = -\sqrt{1 - \cos^2 B} = -\sqrt{1 - (\frac{12}{13})^2} = -\sqrt{1 - \frac{144}{169}} = -\frac{5}{13}$.
7
Use the sum formula: $\sin(A + B) = \sin A \cos B + \cos A \sin B$.
8
$\sin(A + B) = (-\frac{3}{5})(\frac{12}{13}) + (\frac{4}{5})(-\frac{5}{13}) = -\frac{36}{65} - \frac{20}{65} = -\frac{56}{65}$.
