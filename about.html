About DuckMath

We believe every Malaysian student deserves access to great math practice — for free.

Our Mission

DuckMath was founded on a simple belief: the biggest barrier to good exam results in Malaysia isn't intelligence — it's access to quality practice and timely feedback.

Private tuition costs hundreds of ringgit per month. Textbook answer keys tell you what the right answer is, but not why yours was wrong. DuckMath changes that.

By combining a structured question bank with AI-powered grading, we give every student — regardless of family income — instant, personalised feedback on every answer they submit. The duck mascot and level-up system make the process genuinely fun.

Our Story

DuckMath was built by TinkTech, a small education-technology team based in Malaysia. The idea was born from years of watching students struggle not because they lacked effort, but because the feedback loop between "I tried this" and "here's what you did wrong" took days — by which time the lesson was long forgotten.

We started with a simple Streamlit prototype, a Supabase database, and a lot of coffee. Within weeks, students were asking their teachers to add more questions. The duck levels came next — because learning is more fun when there's a ridiculous goal to chase.

Today DuckMath supports SPM and UEC mathematics syllabuses, with questions submitted and verified by experienced Malaysian math teachers.

What We Stand For

Every question is reviewed by a human teacher before going live. AI grades answers; humans ensure the questions are correct.
